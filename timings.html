How to get a direct download URL:

Google Drive:

  1. Upload your audio file to Google Drive
  2. Right-click the file and select "Share"
  3. Set permissions to "Anyone with the link"
  4. Copy the link and paste it here
  5. Our app will automatically convert it to a direct download link

Dropbox:

  1. Upload your audio file to Dropbox
  2. Click "Share" and create a shared link
  3. Copy the link
  4. You can paste it directly here, or
  5. For better compatibility, change '?dl=0' to '?dl=1' at the end of the URL

MediaFire:

  1. Open your file on MediaFire
  2. Right-click on the download button
  3. Select "Copy link address" or "Copy link"
  4. Paste the link here
